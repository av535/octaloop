FAQs

What is IBT25?
IBT25 (India Blockchain Tour 2025) is a nationwide roadshow connecting blockchain enthusiasts, developers, investors, and industry leaders across multiple cities in India. It’s designed to build local communities, spark conversations, and drive the adoption of blockchain technology.
How is IBT25 different from MM25?
IBT25 is a multi-city tour happening across India, engaging local blockchain communities. It serves as a lead-up to Metamorphosis 2025 (MM25), which is our flagship global conference bringing together the biggest names in blockchain, Web3, and crypto for a large-scale event. Think of IBT25 as the on-the-ground movement and MM25 as the grand finale where everything comes together!
When and where is IBT25 happening?
IBT25 will take place across multiple cities in India, starting in May 2025. Check out the city pages for more information.
How can I participate in IBT25?
You can attend IBT25 as a participant, speaker, or sponsor. You can either buy the tickets directly or fill out the inquiry form if you’d like to partner.
Is IBT25 free to attend?
Each IBT25 city stop is a ticketed event. You can explore expert panel discussions, startup pitches, networking opportunities, and valuable insights from top blockchain leaders plus some exciting giveaways! To attend, please visit the city page of your choice and buy your ticket from there.
Will IBT25 speakers also be at MM25?
Many of our top speakers from IBT25 will also be speaking at MM25, alongside global industry leaders.
How do I get updates about IBT25?
Follow us on X for event announcements, schedules, and exclusive content. Need more insights? Chat with our community on Telegram.
I'm a company/startup. How can I collaborate with IBT25?
We offer sponsorship, speaking, and partnership opportunities. Reach out to us to discuss how we can collaborate!
What is the IBT25 Demo Day, and how can startups participate?
IBT25 will feature a Demo Day, where early-stage blockchain startups get the chance to pitch their ideas to top-tier venture capitalists (VCs) and investors. Selected startups can gain exposure, mentorship, and potential funding to scale their projects.

How to participate:

  • Buy the Startup Ticket for MM25 Delhi
  • Send us the pitchdeck at events@octaloop.com
  • You'll get a chance to pitch live in front of leading investors at MM25.
  • The best startups from IBT25 may also be invited to pitch on the main stage at MM25!
