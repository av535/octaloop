We're relaunching the Octaloop Blog — a space for ideas, insights, and honest takes on India's tech ecosystem.

## What to expect

Every few days, we'll be writing about things we're seeing in the market, events we're running, founders we're meeting, and trends we think deserve more attention.

No fluff. No press releases. Just what we actually think.

## Why now

India's blockchain and Web3 ecosystem has matured significantly since we started in 2013. The conversations are sharper, the builders are more serious, and the global interest is real.

We want to document that — and be part of the conversation.

## Stay tuned

More posts coming soon. If you have a topic you'd like us to cover, write to us at [contact@octaloop.com](mailto:contact@octaloop.com).
