Our Greatest Assets
Anupam Varshney
Chief Navigator
Anupam’s crypto journey began when he bought Bitcoin from an anonymous Reddit user in 2013. In pursuit of raising blockchain awareness, he started a community in Delhi where a handful of curious souls would huddle over coffee and argue over block times.

Those small meetups kept snowballing - first into the largest Delhi crypto community, and by 2022 into the nationwide India Blockchain Tour, followed by Metamorphosis.

Nearly a decade on, he still lives for turning strangers into crypto believers - one event, tweet, and espresso shot at a time.
Govind Dhiman
Chief Pixel Wrangler
Govind can hear hex codes whisper. Hand him a half-baked wireframe, and after one contemplative sip of imaginary Goan craft beer, he’ll turn it into a screen you want to lick. In the parallel universe where he actually runs that beach-villa restaurant (menu: vindaloo, zero khichdi), he’s plating UI elements instead of prawns.

Until then, he keeps Octaloop’s brand spicy, renames every rogue Figma layer, and reminds us that great design starts with two ears, one stylus, and a villa-sized dose of chill.
Mrinaleeni Yelam
Deal Whisperer
Powered by Coffee. Is. Survival., Mrinaleeni can spin a one-word “Hey” into a deal memo before her cold-brew drip runs dry. She parachuted into Web3 chasing “free airdrops” on Telegram, stayed for the chaos, and now writes pitch messages so sharp they should carry a safety warning. 
Off the clock, she feeds the crew (literally - never skip her lunch runs) and daydreams of a Wakanda condo with self-folding laundry bots. Tiny triumph? A prospect replying “Let’s chat” to her cold DM - cue silent fist-pump and main-character strut.
Jeevesh Kuril
Partnership Viking
Jeevesh’s origin story: midnight Doge FOMO, one massive white-paper deep dive, and a permanent addiction to high-stakes crypto. With chicken wings, whiskey, and a battered notebook, he can hammer out seven-figure partnerships from orbit - or from Valhalla, which he’d happily mortgage if Odin accepts stablecoins.

He translates DeFi jargon into Wall-Street speak before you finish the sentence “exogenous liquidity,” then reminds you: “Playing it safe never moved mountains.” Just don’t invite him to dish duty; risk is his game, detergent is not.
Simarpreet Kaur
The Showrunner
“Coffee before chaos” is Simarpreet’s operating system. One innocent favor at a college fest snowballed into a career of taming tech fails, speaker meltdowns, and schedules daring gravity. Hand her a broken Google Sheet and watch it return color-coded and crisis-proof. 

She’d happily mortgage a cottage in Stars Hollow - town-hall drama in exchange for perfect fall foliage. Off-duty goal? Represent India in the noble sport of 13-hour naps. Life rule: “Downfall mein bhi masti nhi rukni chahiye.”
Shekhar Chander
Code Sorcerer
Shekhar didn’t choose the crypto life - it chose him. Armed with just a laptop, snacks, and a torch (don’t ask), he now runs the backend of Octaloop’s Web3 spaceship. Fluent in Kumaoni and dad jokes, Shekhar treats clean code like a mic-drop moment. He dreams of owning a jungle-side mortgage with Baloo and Bagheera as neighbors and swears by cooking as therapy - just don’t ask him to do laundry.

The quiet powerhouse of the team, he solves problems with “Abhi theek krke deta hu” energy and keeps the ship flying even when no one remembers signing him up.
Joel Ayaan Sharma
Vibe Keeper
Bold, devoted, caffeinated - Joel juggles Discord threads while bench-pressing dumbbells under mood lighting. Dogecoin memes lured him into Web3; the sheer madness made him stay. He’d take out a Rivendell mortgage for the mountain view, cracks jokes darker than a bear market, and speaks fluent Nepali when crowd control needs a reset.

Micro-win of the day? Skipping carbs without skipping a beat. As he reminds us with MJ-level conviction: “They Don’t Care About Us” - but our community always will.
